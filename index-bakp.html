Mario Kart is a series of go-kart-style racing video games developed and published by Nintendo as spin-offs from its trademark Super Mario series.
The series was created by Shigeru Miyamoto and Takaya Imamura, and has been developed and published by Nintendo since 1992.
The series has been a critical and commercial success, with over 100 million copies sold worldwide.

Mario Kart 8 Deluxe is a kart racing video game and latest installment in the Mario Kart series, developed and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch console.
It is the second Mario Kart game for a home console since Mario Kart: Double Dash!! for the GameCube in 2003.
The game was released worldwide on April 27, 2017.
Mario Kart 8 is the first game in the series to use anti-aliasing, and the first to use HD graphics.
It is also the first game in the series to feature anti-gravity racing, which allows players to drive on walls and ceilings.
The game features a ton of new items, characters, and tracks, as well as the return of many classic characters and tracks from previous games.

This game features different power-ups and items, that can be used to hinder or help other racers.
Perhaps the most important item is the Banana Peel.
It does not only act as an excellent trap to set for other racers, but can also be used as a defensive item, allowing the racer to dodge Red Shells and Green Shells
